About Capgemini

Capgemini is a French multinational company. It provides services in business consulting, information technology and outsourcing.

To know more click on the link : Capgemini

Recruitment Process

Capgemini hires through:-

  • On Campus Drives
  • Off Campus Drives

Profiles

Capgemini hires for profiles including:-

  • Analyst
  • Software Developer
  • Software Engineer

Rounds

For both On Campus and Off Campus drives, Capgemini conducts an online exam, with 4 mandatory sections:

  1. Pseudocode Round
  2. English Communication
  3. Game based aptitude test
  4. Behavioral Competency Test
Each of the sections are eliminators. Upon clearing one section can only the candidates proceed to the next section. And upon clearing the above sections candidates are eligible for interview round. For some selected colleges Capgemini offers a coding round to upgrade their package. Students who perform well in this coding round can give interview for a higher package role.

Interview Process:-

Capgemini conducts one single interview, a combination of Technical + HR Interview.
The Interview is conducted on the Superset platform.

Interview questions

  1. Tell me something about yourself
  2. Difference between C and C++.
  3. What is OOPS concepts?
  4. What is Primary Key?
  5. What is Foreign Key?
  6. Why should we hire you?
  7. Some HR questions.
  8. Are you ready to relocate?
